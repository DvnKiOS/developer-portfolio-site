  • Device Information: Basic device information for technical support and compatibility

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

Privacy Policy for MarkMate - Easy Watermarking

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

MarkMate is designed to respect your privacy:

  • Image Data: Photos you select for watermarking (processed locally on your device)
  • Watermark Templates: Your custom watermark designs and preferences
  • App Usage: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance

All image processing occurs locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to our servers unless you explicitly use cloud backup features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide watermarking functionality and image processing

  • Improve app performance and user interface
  • Offer customer support

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting, but these do not collect personally identifiable information.

5. Your Control

You have full control over your images and can delete them from the app at any time.
